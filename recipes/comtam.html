    • Instructions

    1. On a hot fry pan, sear and cook the pork chop until cooked.
    2. Add you vegetables to the mix and let it sit and pickle at room temperature.
    3. Add the remaining ingredients: lime juice, fish sauce and chilli to complete it.
    4. Fry a sunny side up egg
